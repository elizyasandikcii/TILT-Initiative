EcoCatch

EcoCatch is an all-in-one environmental intelligence platform that unifies ecosystem modeling, biodiversity risk analysis, and conservation tracking. It empowers scientists and environmentalists to simulate ecosystem changes (EcoFrame), assess biodiversity threats (EcoVitals), and monitor the real-time impact of conservation actions (EcoShift) — all from a single, data-driven dashboard.

NeuroPrognos

NeuroPrognos is an AI-based tool that predicts the risk of developing Huntington’s Disease and provides personalized insights for early intervention and therapy planning.

Aether

Aether is an AI-driven fire prevention and suppression system designed for lithium-ion battery environments, especially in enclosed spaces like ships or shopping malls. It monitors, delays, and extinguishes fires caused by overheating through intelligent sensors, advanced materials, and automated control systems — ensuring safety before disaster strikes.

Echoes of World

Echoes of World is a powerful documentary that uncovers the global challenges shaping our planet’s future — from climate change and deforestation to pollution and inequality. Through breathtaking visuals and real human stories, it reveals how environmental, economic, and social issues are deeply connected, inspiring viewers to take action for a sustainable future.
